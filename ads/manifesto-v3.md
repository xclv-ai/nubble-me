# NUBBLE.ME — CAMPAIGN V3
## "We Are All Idiots"

---

## THE MANIFESTO

---

We are all idiots. Not just you.

Not because we're dumb.
Because we stopped reading.

We scroll. We skim. We save articles we'll never open.
We screenshot book recommendations and let them rot in our camera rolls.
We read the headline, maybe the first paragraph, then bounce.

We used to read 300-page books on a Tuesday.
Now we can't get through a 7-minute article without checking our phone.

This isn't a hot take. It's a diagnosis.

We lost the skill.

---

And the worst part?
We know.

We feel it. The creeping guilt every time we abandon something halfway.
The sense that we're getting dumber — not because the information isn't there,
but because we've lost the patience to absorb it.

We built the entire internet.
And we can't sit still long enough to read what's on it.

---

Nobody's coming to save us.

The books won't get shorter. The authors won't rewrite for your attention span.
The world isn't going to slow down and wait for you to finish Chapter 3.

And no — AI summaries aren't reading. They're someone else's understanding
poured into your head. You didn't earn it. You won't remember it.
You'll forget it by lunch.

---

So what do we actually need?

Not shorter content. Not dumber content. Not AI-chewed content.

We need a way back in.

A door that opens at whatever width you can handle today.
Three minutes? Fine. Start there. Get the skeleton of every idea.
Feel something pull you? Swipe deeper. Go as far as you want.

Not a summary someone else made.
The actual book. The actual article. The actual ideas.
Just — at the depth your brain can handle right now.

---

That's nubble.

Not a replacement for reading. A booster.

The thing that gets you from "I'll read it later" to actually reading it.
From a screenshot in your camera roll to an idea in your head.
From feeling like an idiot to being the person who actually read the thing.

Scroll to move through ideas. Swipe to control depth.
Start shallow. Go deeper when you're ready.

---

Your attention span isn't broken.
It's just been starved of content that meets it where it is.

**nubble.me**
Stop being an idiot. Start reading again.

## ORIGIN STORY (V3 — "The Intervention")

This is an intervention.

You used to be a reader. You used to lose entire afternoons in a book.
You used to think for fun.

Now you save articles like a squirrel hoarding nuts for a winter that never comes.
Your Pocket account is a museum of good intentions.
Your Kindle shows 47 books at 3% progress.

You're not lazy. You're not stupid. You just forgot how to start.

Nubble is the door. Not a smaller book — a wider entrance.
Start with the skeleton. The nub of every idea, one line each.
Ninety seconds. That's it.

Then the magic: something will pull you. A section. A phrase. An idea
that hooks into your brain. Swipe right. Go deeper.

You didn't need a shorter book.
You needed a way back in.
