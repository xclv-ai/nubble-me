## WHY "NUBBLE" IS A PERFECT NAME

### The Etymology Is the Product

**nub** (n., 1834) — "the point, gist, or heart of something."

That's literally what the app does. Every swipe extracts the nub — the essential point — or expands beyond it. The name carries the core value proposition inside its own etymology.

**nubble** (n.) — "a small knob or lump."

A nubble is a small, tactile thing. Something you feel under your thumb. The app is built on swiping — a thumb gesture. The physicality is baked in.

**nubble** (v., obsolete) — "to beat with the fist."

From Low German *nubben* ("to knock, cuff"). There's a raw, punchy energy to the word. You don't gently browse a nubble. You punch into it.

## PHONETIC NEIGHBORS

### nubble × nibble
> "Don't just nibble content. Nubble it."

**nibble** = "to bite gently; eat by gnawing off small bits" (c. 1500)
**nubble** = to get the nub of something

One letter apart. Nibble is passive grazing. Nubble is active choosing.

### nubble × knuckle
> "Reading with your knuckles, not your eyes."

**knubble** (the archaic cousin) = "to handle with thumbs and knuckles, as in kneading dough." That's literally swiping. You knead through content with your thumb.

### nubble × humble
> "A humble reader for readers who aren't."

## THE LIGHTHOUSE CONNECTION

**Nubble Light** — Cape Neddick Light Station in York, Maine. One of the most photographed lighthouses in the world. Sits on a small rocky island called "the Nubble."

The parallel writes itself:

> A lighthouse extracts signal from the dark.
> Nubble extracts signal from the noise.

> "The Nubble has guided ships for 150 years.
> nubble.me guides readers now."

The rocky island was called "the Nubble" because of its shape — a small knob jutting out of the ocean. A protuberance. Something that stands out.

That's what depth-0 content is: the part that juts out. The signal rising above the sea.

## THE GOLD CONNECTION

**Nub** in ancient Egyptian = "gold" (nb / nub). The land south of Egypt was called **Nubia** — "land of gold."

> "Every text has gold in it. Nubble finds it."
